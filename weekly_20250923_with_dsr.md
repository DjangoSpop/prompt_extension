### 2025-09-15 – Tonj North – Assault 321578
**Location:** Awul 

- **Source:** Manual Entry
- **Narrative:**
2. CATTLE RAID IN TONJ NORTH On 15 Sept, UNMISS received information from several sources in Awul Payam, Tonj North County, that on the same date (15 Sept) at about 1400hrs, suspected armed Jurbol youths from Rualbet Payam attacked a group of Dinka Atok herders who were grazing their cattle and goats in Walkou village in Awul Payam targeting the livestock. The attack is considered an isolated revenge attack within the context of the subsided communal violence between the two communities. The attackers shot and injured 01 adult male Dinka Atok civilian, seized and stole at least 16 goats and escaped with them back into Rualbet Payam. It is reported that Atok herders were able to quickly flee away with their cattle and escaped the reach of the attackers. Armed Atok youths reportedly responded well late after the attackers had long fled away. The injured Atok civilian is reportedly stable on treatment at the Warrap Town PHCC where he was evacuated. SOURCE FIOC DSR (SAGE No: 321578)
